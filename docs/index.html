    About me

    When I first encountered coding, I wasn’t exactly thrilled. The classes felt dull, and I couldn’t see why I should even bother learning it. But as I’ve grown, I’ve developed a fascination for creating and bringing my ideas to life.

    The problem? I kept running into roadblocks. The software I found on the market never quite fit my needs. After countless moments of frustration (and a fair amount of head-to-keyboard action), I finally said, “Screw it! I’m going to learn to create whatever software or programme I need myself. Why wait for someone else to make it for me?”

    And here I am now, fully immersed in the world of coding—still occasionally hitting my head against the keyboard, but loving every minute of the journey.
